EXAMPLES - Abstract artworks:

Example 1:
A drawing with black ink and flowy brush strokes coming together to form various round and curved shapes, resembling waves or grassy fields.

Example 2:
Nine rows of flowy, intertwined drawings of shapes resembling handwriting, drawn in black ink against a light background.

Example 3:
An oil painting with energetic brush strokes in colors of black, red, orange, green, and white. The brush strokes overlap in both flowy and jagged motions.

Example 4:
Geometric flat shapes of vibrant colors depict a group of six figures painting at easels.

Example 5:
A painting of a vast gray, barren landscape with a few small, blob-like figures in both the background and foreground. In the foreground, there are three figures and the two on our left are each connected to two strings that attach to another figure or object in the background.
