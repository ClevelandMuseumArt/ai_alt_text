EXAMPLES - Artworks with text:

Example 1:
A lithograph print featuring bold black letters spelling "HOPE" across the center. The rounded, sans-serif letters fill most of the composition against a red background with a thin blue border.

Example 2:
A photograph of a coffee can with a yellow lid. The cylindrical metal container is labeled "Savarin" in red script lettering with "COFFEE" in smaller black capitals below it.

Example 3:
An ink and color hanging scroll showing Japanese calligraphy arranged in vertical columns. The brushstrokes vary from thick to thin, creating visual rhythm across the cream-colored paper.

Example 4:
A woven textile flag with areas of discoloration forming large letters that spell out "SOILED" across the stripes. The faded sections contrast against the brighter red and white bands.

Example 5:
A woodblock print depicting a tire with the letter "V" and number "8" superimposed at the center of the hub. The angular, industrial-style characters are rendered in stark black against the gray rubber.
