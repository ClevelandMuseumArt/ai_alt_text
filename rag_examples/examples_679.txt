A black lekythos ceramic vessel features a narrow body thinning into a cylindrical neck with a funnel-like lip. On the body, orange detailing depicts a bearded man in profile, pulling strands of curly hair in front of his face with one hand and holding a sword in the other. A crested helmet sits on a stool in front of him. Bands of rectangular spirals frame the scene, with a floral pattern transitioning to the neck.
