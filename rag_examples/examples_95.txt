A vertically oriented print depicts a white and black figure like two cloths shaped by pointy ears and nose, hinting at a coyote underneath. The cloths are covered with black ink splotches and ripple as they float in front of an ombré teal blue to pale pink background. White four-pointed stars hover in the upper left corner, and blue lines and circles swish down to the figure's right. Gray ink splotches are splattered below the floating cloths.
