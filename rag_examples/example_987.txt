A hanging scroll with gray ink washes depicts the monk Niaoge in a tree along the scroll's left edge. The rest of the composition is the pale sandy-brown color of the scroll. Depicted in light gray strokes, the round-bodied Niaoge sits, hands on his knees, looking down from the treetop. To the right of the tree trunk in the lower-left corner runs a column of Japanese text (see "Inscriptions").
