A painted wood panel fragment depicts stylized, flattened people in profile. A mummified man and woman with medium and light skin tones stand facing our left, their bodies tightly wrapped. A woman with light skin tone crouches in front of them, reaching for the man's wrapped legs. Left, a man with medium-dark skin tone and pleated white and leopard print garments holds out two objects. Hieroglyphics, a pictorial writing system, extend in columns above.
