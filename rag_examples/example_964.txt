A horizontally long beige and gray silk and metal textile with two rows of four triangular, shield shapes, is missing fragments of the cloth, particularly on the right side. In the upper row, shields with repeated white "T" shapes alternate with shields with silver fleur-de-lis shapes. In the lower row, they alternate between a gray and metal checker pattern and a silver with a dark brown column, three protrusions angling up on the left and one on the right.
