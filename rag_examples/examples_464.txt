A circular tempera painting depicts a nude man looking and reaching out toward a person wrapped in white with a gold halo floating in on a cloud against a solid blue sky. Below, a central green tree with red dots leans right in a pale brown landscape. A white bird, blue, orange, and brown four-legged animals, and a small, curled-up creature with pointy ears all face the nude man. A gray-green stripe encircles the painting.
