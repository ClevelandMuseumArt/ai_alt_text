A cylindrical wood cup widens subtly at the rim. Three bands of images, mainly pigmented with red, black, and cream, decorate the cup. The top band depicts a stylized figure in a tunic holding a spear, another figure with an ax to our right. Below is a narrower band with geometric shapes. The bottom band contains three rows of insectlike creatures. Cracks wind through the paint, with flakes chipped off, particularly across the bottom.
