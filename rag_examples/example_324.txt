A horizontally oriented graphite drawing painted in rich pastel colors depicts a highly geometric scene of figures painting at triangular, yellow-brown easels that pyramid up across the scene. The six figures, five with dark-pink skin and one dark skin tone, four of their artworks visible, paint primarily in pinks, cream, and black. The figure furthest to our left holds paintbrushes behind their back, working on a still life while the figure to the left of them paints a chess board.
