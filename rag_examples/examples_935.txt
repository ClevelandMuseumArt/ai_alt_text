A hanging scroll depicts men with light to medium-light skin tones hunting on horseback among a landscape sparsely outlined in black against a yellow-brown background. Most have horses in brown shades and wear green, orange, or blue robes. One man wears purple, riding a white horse. Three mounted men and a white dog surround a person who has dismounted in the lower half. Two race ahead in the upper half, one with a white bird on his arm, the other throwing his arms in the air toward two flying, white birds.
