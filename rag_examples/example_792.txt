A barong, a single-edged leaf-shaped sword, features a silver metal blade. A cylindrical bronze handle extends up and curves to the left, transitioning to braided bands and capped with a dark-brown piece. A narrow strip extends across the top in an arcing diagonal line, the upper right side branching into curling scrolls. On the piece's body runs a pattern of alternating white triangles and two stacked dots. A winding, vine-like yellow-brown pattern decorates the blade.
