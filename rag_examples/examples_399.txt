A horizontally oriented print in black ink depicts skeletons and skeletal people highlighted in fine cross-hatching against a dark cross-hatched background. In the center, a skeleton lies on a rocky outcropping with people gripping its head and kneeling before it. Standing over it and holding a book, a winged skeleton looks to our left at another skeletal person. On the far left, one skeletal person leans on a cane and holds their arm toward the winged skeleton.
