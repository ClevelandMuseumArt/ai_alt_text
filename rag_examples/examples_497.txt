A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts two people with light skin tones at a shrine in front of a tree at the edge of water. Both wear white robes fastened with gold sashes. The person on our left stands. The other, a gold halo around their head, kneels before the shrine. They offer a yellow garland, two coconuts, and pink flowers with green leaves at the base of the tree where a four-headed gold snake coils.
