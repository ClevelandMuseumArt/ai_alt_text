Light grey-brown rock panels feature a relief of a muscular, winged figure in profile, facing our left. The figure's right arm bends up, their palm facing us. Their left arm extends slightly forward and down, holding a branch with flowers. They wear an ankle-length robe and have a rectangular beard and hair finely detailed with waves and spirals. Constructed from three panels, on atop the other, text scrawls over the carvings on the middle panel.
