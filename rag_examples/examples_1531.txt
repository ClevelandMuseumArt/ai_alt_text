A vertically oriented naturalistic oil painting depicts Saint Ambrose, a man with light skin tone and bushy, brown beard, seated writing in an open book and wearing bishop's garments. These include gold and lace fringed silky white robes and a matching, pointed, shield-shaped headdress. Contrasting with a solid, dark background, he looks up to our right, lips parted. He holds a quill-like writing utensil in his right hand, holding the book open with his left.
