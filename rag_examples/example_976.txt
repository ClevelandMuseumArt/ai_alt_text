A hanging scroll depicts celestial beings descending on light brown clouds against a dark brown sky, a full moon in the upper left. In the upper half the human-like beings extend diagonally down right to left, wearing layered robes with gold patterns, some beings half the size of others. Arcing right to left in the lower half, the beings also have halos, three of them with red skin and wraps around their waists in contrast with the other beings with light skin tones and wearing robes.
