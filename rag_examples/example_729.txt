A vertically oriented tapestry is covered in winding, long-bodied dragons in shades of orange, blue, and green against a dark purple-brown background. Teeth bared, a spiral-patterned, circular pearl appears before each dragon, a singular, branching, flamelike shape extending from each pearl. Filling in the space between the dragons are swirling clouds or puffy smoke shapes. The shapes between the dragon alternate between the same colors as the dragons themselves.
