A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts two armed forces with medium-dark to light skin tones meeting in front of the pink-brown walls of a citadel. People look over the edge of the wall, squared buildings behind them, and two men on black horses ride over the moat bridge. In the lower half, one army curves down from the bridge. A man wearing a blue tunic, arrows at his hip, gestures to another on a white horse in a black tunic. Both armies have gold helmets and detailing over tunics in various colors.
