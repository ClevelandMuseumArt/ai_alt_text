A dark-brown-black wood club shaped like a spade has a cylindrical handle twice the length of the spade. The spade shape is patterned with repeated fine triangles intersecting to make larger triangles and other geometric shapes. A cross cuts through these patterns. Tightly grouped horizontal lines extend up the handle, occasionally interrupted by rows of triangles. At the very edge of the handle runs vertical rows of serrated lines. The handle flares slightly at the end.
