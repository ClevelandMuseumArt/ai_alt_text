A vertically long silk textile features a central band of dark blue, red, yellow, and green ornate intertwining lines and organic shapes. Flanking, panels with a red grid frame smaller panels with fine winding yellow patterns and Arabic text. On either side, three rectangles mark the yellow's center with a red, vaguely floral, radiating pattern. Above the grids run two bands and below one featuring winding patterns. Arabic text intertwines with the geometric designs (see "Inscriptions").
