A dark gray schist rock is carved with a scene of Siddhartha, a man wearing a rippling robe, his hair in a bun, and a halo behind his head, standing beside a tree, branches and leaves clustered in a circle atop. An altar stands at the tree's base, a figure with their head and arms broken off rising from it and another, also heavily chipped, kneeling facing Siddhartha below. People gather mostly on our right.
