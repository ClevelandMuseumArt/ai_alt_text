A realistic horizontally oriented oil painting depicts two, brown-feathered peregrine falcons facing each other on a rocky outcropping that slopes up from the lower left corner of the painting, a light gray-blue sky behind them. The left falcon pins a duck under its left talon, wings flapping and open beak turned toward us. Seen from the side, the right falcon's wings are folded as it leans down, talons pinning another duck and blood dripping from its open beak.
