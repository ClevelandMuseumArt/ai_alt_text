A hanging scroll depicts a tigress, two tiger cubs, and a leopard cub in front of intertwining pine tree trunks. The needled, gray tree branches in the upper right partially obscure a red-orange sun in the upper center. The tigress, facing our left, turns her head over her shoulder, teeth bared. She and the leopard have fine, subtly yellow-tinged fur patterned in black while the tiger cubs appear gray. All have circular, gold eyes with black dots for pupils.
