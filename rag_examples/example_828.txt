A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts a room. On the right, Antiochus, a man, lies under red bedsheets while his father stands over him in a cream tunic. This scene is mostly cast in shadow. A diagonal light focuses on the left side of the painting, where Stratonice, a woman wearing a light-pink tunic, leans against a gray column, chin resting on the back of her right hand as she looks down. All have medium-light skin tones.
