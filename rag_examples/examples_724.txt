A horizontally oriented tapestry depicts a garden scene with people with light skin tones wearing various shades of pink, and dark-blue garments that wrap them in layered folds, the ground covered in small flowers. In the center, a woman sits with a book open in her lap as two winged figures hold a crown over her head. Below, a woman and two children a fourth of the central woman's size move among the flowers. An Old French inscription cuts in on the upper right.
