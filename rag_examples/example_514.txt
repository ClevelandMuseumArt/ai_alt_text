A gray sandstone sculpture depicts Ganesha, a god with a human body and elephant head, ears flaring on either side. Ganesha wears a short sampot, a cloth wrapped around the waist and falling just over the knees, the lower legs broken off below. Parts of both arms are missing. Ganesha's right hand clutches a radish against the side of his belly. His trunk hangs down and bends toward his left hand, partially broken away.
