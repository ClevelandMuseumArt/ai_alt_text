A horizontally oriented tapestry depicts a garden scene with people with light skin tones wearing dark-pink, light-pink, and dark-blue garments that enwrap them in layered folds, the ground covered in small flowers. Central, a woman sits with a book open in her lap and two winged figures holding a crown over her head. Six more surround her, playing instruments or singing. Below, a woman and two children a fourth of the central woman's size move among the flowers. An Old French inscription cuts in on the upper right (see "Inscriptions").
