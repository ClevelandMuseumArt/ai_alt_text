A settee, a long seat, features scarlet red, rectangular cushions upholstered on the seat and back within an ornate gilt wood structure. Ram heads protrude from the front edge of each arm and pinecones stand on top of either side of the settee's back. The settee back arches up, filled with various patterns of representations of flowers, leaves, and curling vines. Bundles like tassels cinched at the bottom hang from the base.
