A larger-than-life carved, wood statue depicts Guanyin, a deity standing with his hips swaying to his right, right arm broken off and left arm broken off above the elbow. He looks slightly down, a murky circle inset into his forehead, and hair pulled up with several faces in it, most broken away. He wears a multilayered, blue and red wrap around his waist, chest bare save for a wrapping over his left shoulder and a necklace.
