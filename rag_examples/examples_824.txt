A vertically oriented oil painting with soft brushstrokes creates a hazy scene of a woman bending down facing our right to show a basket of apples to a woman seated on the side of a dirt road, two children seated next to her, all with light skin tone. The apple seller's clothes are muted, contrasting with the woman's white and children's pink and blue dresses. In the lower left, a small dog looks up at the apples. Dappled light bathes the scene, hazy trees and greenery in the background.
