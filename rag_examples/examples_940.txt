A white, square, cotton and silk cloth colorfully embroidered depicts three rows of shrines with rounded, pyramidal tops and square bases in which sit Hindu gods with light, blue, or red skin tones. In a central shrine double the size of the others stands the four-armed, blue-skinned god Vishnu wearing a yellow skirt and two red strips hanging. Two women with light skin tone on either side, face him and hold offerings. Around the border run blue, red, white, green, and yellow quatrefoils, shapes like four-petaled flowers.
