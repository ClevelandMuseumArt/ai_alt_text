A horizontally oriented oil painting against a dark sky depicts Venus, a blond-haired goddess with light skin tone leaning over the corpse of Adonis, a man with a pasty, light skin tone, a single flower falling from Venus toward his body. Adonis lies upside down from our angle on a red cloth, arms spread straight, and a gash on his bare chest. Venus steps forward, arms thrown in the air and muted yellow and blue clothes covering her left breast. Child-like putti and animals surround them.
