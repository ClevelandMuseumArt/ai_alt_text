A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a towering, ruined building with crumbling beige walls with windows and archways, against a cloudy, gray sky, waves cast in darkness in the lower left. Muted sunrays from cracks in the clouds illuminate the ruins. The tallest section of the ruins juts up in the center, on which black birds perch. From within the building on the right, three fluffy, narrow trees sway to the right, black birds flying behind them.
