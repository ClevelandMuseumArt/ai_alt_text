A collage depicts two people standing at the foot of the pedestal for the Cleveland Museum of Art statue of The Thinker. The statue depicts a nude man seated resting his chin on his fist. The collage is created from fragments of black-and-white family photos pasted together. People's faces show throughout the collage, especially on the pedestal and building behind the statue. Other parts of photographs are fragmented beyond recognition. Some show scrawls of writing.
