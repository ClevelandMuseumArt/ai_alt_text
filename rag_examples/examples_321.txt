A ten-panel folding screen of a bird's-eye view depicts the Korean city of Pyongyang with a brown silk background and the city painted with black ink, occasional buildings outlined in red and green. The triangular outlines of houses cluster in the center, flanked on either side and outside city walls by trees and farmland. In the foreground, people maneuver boats through a port while, in the background curves a mountain range. Korean calligraphy is interspersed throughout.
