A horizontally oriented tempera painting depicts two scenes behind a yellow-gold wall, one within a square, gold building on the left and another in a garden on the right. Central to both scenes, a gray-skinned, black-bearded, 10-headed and 20-armed demon Ravana holds various weapons. Left, blue or red-skinned horned minions encircle him. On the right, he stands before a woman kneeling under a tree, red, yellow-orange, and black skinned tusked figures behind her and two women behind him.
