A handscroll depicts women with light skin tones and white faces in a palace in the central three-fifths. Red and brown beams and gates support the palace, porches wrapping around and filled with women with black hair wearing long-sleeved robes. They also walk into the garden, dotted with trees and with water in the upper left. Staggered columns of Chinese calligraphy flank on the left, and on the right, a large row of Chinese calligraphy.
