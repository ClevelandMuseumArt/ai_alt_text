A gold pendant depicts a figure with an animal-like head and human body with squared limbs and a narrow torso. Their mouth is open, revealing fangs and a sticking out tongue, short antlers protrude from the top of their head, and intertwined serpents extend across the top of the head like hair, a serpent head resting on each shoulder. Gaps in the upper arms, torso, and thighs are each filled with a singular spiral.
