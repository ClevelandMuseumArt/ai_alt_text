A vertically oriented, partially abstract watercolor and opaque gouache painting with swirling and streaking blotches of red, light blue, black, and white reveals printed people. Center right, against a swath of plain white paper, a profile of a human head with medium-light skin tone faces our left, wearing a crown, beige cutouts of various animals scattered beneath them. Upper left, two rows of blotchy, blue faces with pink features and black hair emerge from red paint swirls.
