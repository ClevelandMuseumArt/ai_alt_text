Two sides of a gold coin feature an inscription circling an image on both sides (see "Inscriptions" field). On the left, two rows of wavy petals surrounding seeds suggest a rose, crowned and flanked by a crowned "h" and "k." On the right, is a crowned coat of arms divided into four sections. The upper left and lower right sections feature three stylized fleur-de-lis lilies and the remaining sections three elongated and barely discernible lions.
