Two near identical black-and-white square photographs with upper corners rounded, depict Tamil Gateway from a side angle, the second photograph on the right slightly darker. From behind layers of short, light-colored buildings with thatched canopies extends the towering, ornate pyramid-shaped gate twice their height outlined against a clear sky. The gateway is carved with dense layers of columns, figures, and squared off corners that narrow slightly toward the top of the gateway's rectangular column.
