A vertically oriented oil painting with defined but visible brushstrokes depicts a person standing on the rooftop of a red brick, squared building in the lower third and in front of a gray-blue sky in the upper two-thirds. The figure has a medium-dark skin tone and faces the right, wearing a white shirt and dark trousers. Two, well-defined, fluffy white clouds float in the sky, one painted just above the person's head. More building tops are in the background.
