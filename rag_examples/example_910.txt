A vertically oriented oil painting is composed of thin, brushy layers of paint and muted browns and blues save for the red-clothed Abraham sacrificing Isaac with a nude, child-like angel flying above, all three with light skin tones. Isaac twists and steps forward with his right leg, while his left kneels on a pedestal Abraham straddles, holding Isaac's hands behind his back with one hand and swinging a knife back in the other. Abraham turns to look up over his left shoulder at the angel, who places a hand on his shoulder. Behind, a rocky landscape features a tree on our left and an unpainted silhouette of a donkey on our right.
