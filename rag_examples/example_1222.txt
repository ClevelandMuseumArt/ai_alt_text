A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Saint James the Greater, a bearded man holding a staff in his left hand and raising his right. He wears a blue robe with a red sash. He looks down at Hermogenes, a man bowing before him on his knees and elbows, hands clasped in front of his face. To James's left, a shirtless man falls back, looking up. More people gesticulate behind, including on the balcony of a background building. All have light skin tones.
