A horizontally oriented, stylized oil painting depicts a dark park illuminated by light from a carousel in the upper center. With a tented top, it tilts to the left as streaking, pastel paintstrokes suggest the leaping model horses as it turns. Depicted in Euclid Beach Park, the dark mostly casts the groups of people in shadow along with the overhang and trees that frame the carousel left and right. Strings of lights dot the background.
