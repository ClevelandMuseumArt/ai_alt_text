A dark-brown staff has red and orange undertones. Four-fifths of the staff is a narrow cylindrical pole on top of which two carvings of people face out and away from each other, their bodies bending back where their legs would begin and fusing together in a "U" shape behind them. Their disproportionately small hands rest on their stomachs. On top, a baboon braces two long legs on each of the flat caps the people wear.
