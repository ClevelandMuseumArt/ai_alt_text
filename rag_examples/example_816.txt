A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a woman seated in profile on grass, turning to look at us and holding a nude child in her lap, another lying at her side, all with light skin tones. The woman, dressed in white shirt and red skirt extends her legs straight in front of her, the second child lying against them dressed in white shirt and brown pants. Trees mostly in shadow, a gap in the green and orange leaves at the center right shows the dome of a basilica on the horizon.
