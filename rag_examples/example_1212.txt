A vertically oriented oil painting depicts the interior of the Pantheon, a domed structure with a hole showing cloudy, blue sky in the center. The hole concentrates a circle of light on the dome's interior, between the dome's sunken square pattern and the orange-brown columns lining the walls. People barely taller than the columns' bases gather in groups across the Pantheon's interior. White statues intersperse with the columns and, at the far center, doors stand open.
