A vertically oriented oil painting depicts an intertwining column of nude bodies with light skin tone rising in the lower left. A nude person with medium-light skin tone leans up and back at the top of the column, reaching for a woman with light skin tone floating above him wearing rippling garments, hugging herself and looking out at us. From the dark gray clouds, one of several angels with light skin tones reaches down for her. Left, another winged person holds weighing scales, while, two more winged figures stand against a blue and yellow sky along the upper edge, one holding a sword.
