A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a central candle illuminating two women bending over a sleeping man, all with light skin tones. Samson, the man, is shirtless and leans on the lap of Delilah as she cuts a section of his ear-length brown hair. On our left, the other woman holds a candle as she looks to our left and raises a finger to her lips. Light illuminates the sitting woman's face and hands, the rest receding to shadow.
