A vertically oriented oil painting depicts the countryside with a cluster of vibrant vignettes in the lower half and a pink path extending through an arch in the upper half. Colorful and lanky stylized figures with light skin tones created with thick swaths of paint sit in armchairs, contort their bodies, eat, and more. Slightly off center, a figure in a pink dress, facing us, looks up at a figure in a suit, back to us.
