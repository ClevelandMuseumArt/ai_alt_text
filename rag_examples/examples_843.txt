A horizontally oriented tempera painting on a brown wood panel depicts Christ and God sitting on a bench, a dove flying inside a blue, eight-point star between them and against the gold background. From round-arched windows in flanking blue towers, bearded men unroll scrolls. All figures have medium-light skin tones, gold halos, and identifying labels in red text over their heads (see "Inscriptions"). The gold and paint have completely worn away in the upper right corner.
