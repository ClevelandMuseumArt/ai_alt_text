A sleeved cotton and camelid fiber tunic laid flat features rectangular sleeves that jut out at the sides and a narrow "V"-shaped slit in the middle. Alternating vertical bands of medium brown and a serrated spiral pattern interlocking in muted light pink, cream, and shades of green run across the tunic. At the base of each band, a pattern like the silhouette of three steps cuts in, connecting to a cream band and switching directions at the center.
