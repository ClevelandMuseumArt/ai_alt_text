A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts an abstract and sparse forest with angular, mossy-patch covered trees framed against blotchy blue sky. Left, three purple stalks curve into each other. Right, rises a straight, thick orange-brown trunk. Center and half the height stands a tree with orange-brown triangular protrusions, roots digging into a ground with as many mossy patches as the trees. A white, bat-like creature with one pointed wing twice the other's length flies across the center.
