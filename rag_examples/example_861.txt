A vertically long tempera painting depicts two scenes. In the top half, Saint Andrew wearing a brown wrap is bound to a cross by rope winding around his arms and torso and extending into the hands of two flanking men. In the lower half, Saint Francis in brown monk's robes stands next to the pink-and-blue robed Saint Paul, both raising their right hand and grasping a book with their left. Yellow-orange buildings stand behind both scenes. Everyone featured has light skin tones.
