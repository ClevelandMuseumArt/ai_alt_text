A floor mosaic fragment in muted colors depicts Adam and Eve, a nude man and woman with light skin tone, covering themselves with large leaves held in their left hands and clasping the same red fruit in their right. A tree and flowers extend in from the left, where the rest of the mosaic has broken away. A Greek inscription runs across the top (see "Inscriptions"). Cream tesserae, mosaic pieces, create the background in semi-circular arches.
