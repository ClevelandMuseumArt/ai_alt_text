A mixed media sculpture looks down a cartoon-like bustling New York street. A yellow cab, bus, and red car flanked by people on sidewalks jut out front. Behind, teetering buildings narrow before meeting at a perspective-giving point. An orange car carrier and polka-dot cement truck pass each other at a streetlight in the middle-ground. Signs in puffy lettering say things such as "COFFEE" and "DAVES LUNCHEON CORNER" as they jut out from the multi-colored buildings.
