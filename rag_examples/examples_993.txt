A densely patterned blue, white, and gold ceramic-tile prayer niche is set into the center of a rectangular section of wall. Along the rectangular dark blue edges runs a band of swirling white Arabic text (see "Inscriptions") over spiraling turquoise vines with gold and green leaves. Different vine and flower patterns surround the niche and extend into it, with three pointed arches like windows on its upper half and flowers encased in geometric designs below. A white-and-gold band borders the niche.
