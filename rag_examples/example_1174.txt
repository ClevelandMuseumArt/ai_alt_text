A sculpture made from a matte black stone called graywacke depicts a man kneeling and holding in his lap a shrine, a rectangular box containing a carving of a deity. He has smooth, shoulder-length hair and is shirtless. The shrine features a deity wearing a close-fitting cap with a cylindrical beard and holding a scepter. Hieroglyphics, a pictorial writing system, run in a rectangle around the shrine and in a band across the sculpture's pedestal.
