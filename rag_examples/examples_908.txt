A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Calliope, a muse with light skin tone, seated with her right hand holding a board over which paper with Greek text rolls. She holds a funnel-shaped, gold trumpet in her left hand and looks to our right, brown hair billowing toward the portrait bust of a bearded man behind her. She wears a white, rippling dress, with green and blue drapery. Grey-brown rocks at her bare feet morph into a cloudy sky.
