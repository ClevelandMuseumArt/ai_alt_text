A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts a woman on the balcony of a square building on the left side, one finger raising toward her lips, her other hand gesturing to a man below on the right, holding flowers. Both have light skin tones. The man wears a red hat and pants and sheer top, the woman a green top, red head-covering, and gold and pearl jewelry. A dark sky swirls with gray in the background, a grid of flower beds across the bottom.
