A gilt bronze statue depicts the seated goddess Uma, most of the gilding worn away to red-brown. She sits, legs crossed, leaning back on her hands and shifting her weight to her right as well as looking to her right. Turquoise blue stones, some missing, are inset into her necklace, bands around her arms, the band of the wrap around her waist, and, along with semitranslucent circles, her three-pronged headdress. She is topless and wears large hoop earrings.
