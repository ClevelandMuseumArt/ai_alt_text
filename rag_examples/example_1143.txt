A horizontally oriented tempera painting depicts figures seated in grassy clearings surrounded by two circles of various tree types, some sprouting red or pink flowers. In the left clearing, Radha and her confidante, both women with light skin tones, sit facing each other, the confidante pointing toward the trees in the center out of which Krishna, a god with gray-blue skin and a gold crown peers. Krishna repeats in the clearing on the right, holding leaves.
