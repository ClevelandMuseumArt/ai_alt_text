A pair of dark brown sandals with flat soles is elevated by rounded blocks at the heel and toe. The top of the sole is carved in a scalloped, looping design, different on each shoe, while the blocks below the sole have slightly curved stripes extending from the sole to floor.  As a grip for the toes, a peg shaped like an antelope head with pink-tinged glass beads for eyes, mouth open like it's smiling, rises from the sole.
