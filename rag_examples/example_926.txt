A horizontally oriented pastel drawing is covered in frenzied lines in neon pink, yellow, green, and orange from which emerge the sketchy faces of two African American women, side-by-side. Black sketchily outlines their facial features on a white base. On our left, yellow swirls around the face. Right, yellow streaks down the face's side. Another line and a circular eye outline in dark blue color the inner side of each face.
