A set of three, side-by-side prints features three variations of an iron imprint on the page. Central, the iron's brown scorch marks white paper. Left, the iron's shape overlays the close-cropped, grainy, grayscale photo of artist Willie Cole, a Black man with medium-dark skin tone, looking at us. Right, a simplified, bird's-eye rendition of an iron with a wooden handle has been placed over the image of Cole's face, which has been inverted.
