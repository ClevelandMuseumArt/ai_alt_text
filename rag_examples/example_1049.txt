A handscroll features eleven fine-lined illustrations in black ink followed by verses in Chinese calligraphy. In the first illustration, a bearded person wears robes and holds a scroll. Then, armored people emerge from shrubbery. Next, a person rides a feline animal away from a tree. Then, a person rides a turtle over waves and a person stands on spiraling clouds. The remaining six drawings feature pairs of people standing on spiraling clouds.
