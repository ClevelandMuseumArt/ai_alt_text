A vertically oriented embroidered band depicts a light-green grapevine winding over a gold embroidered background, making a column of three roughly oval sections, a stem curving inside each on which a person with light skin tone sits. Each person wears blue and green robes, those in the upper and lower sections wearing cloth hats and have beards, while the person in the center wears a gold crown. The grape clusters are a fifth the leaves' size.
