A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a woman seated on the ground, head tilted back, eyes and mouth open, a sleeping child nestled in her lap. Both have light skin tones. The woman leans back against a beige building where it transitions to a brown, wood-plank fence, a pile of sticks at her back. A light dusting of snow edges the fence and covers the ground. Thick brushstrokes texture the scene, particularly the child.
