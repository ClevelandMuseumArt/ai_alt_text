A vertically oriented print of black ink on pale cream paper depicts Saint Anthony, a man with light skin tone, crown balding, wavy beard, and a monk robe, looking down to our right. Nine reptile-looking monsters encircle him, tugging at him as he hovers mid-air. Different amalgamations of animal body parts create the monsters with wings, horns, scales, tails, fur, and more. They bare their teeth and tug at him, some raising sticks in the air.
