A horizontally oriented tempera painting features a row of seated men, four on each side, with light to medium-light skin tones, facing each other. All have white or black facial hair and wear sheer garments except the man on our central right who wears a dark green robe patterned with red dots encircled by white dots. A red-orange band runs behind the men, a row of trees branching out from it into an upper dark blue band.
