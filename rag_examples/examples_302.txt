A cylindrical ceramic vessel slightly taller than it is wide depicts figures outlined in black with orange accessories against a cream background. On the left, a figure holds a spear pointing down, wearing a black-dotted shirt and headdress with a human face represented on it. On the right, a more plainly dressed figure puts another in a headlock. Orange banners with hieroglyphs, a symbolic writing system, hang between figures. Cream on yellow hieroglyphs rim the vessel.
