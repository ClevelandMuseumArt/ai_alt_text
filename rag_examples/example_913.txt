A vertically oriented oil painting in muted colors depicts the god Apollo and muse Urania, both with light skin tones and wavy, blonde hair. Apollo stands center, a lyre, a hand-held harp, in the crook of his left arm while his right arm holds a laurel wreath to the side, his eyes following it. To our right, Urania leans on a starry orb floating on clouds, gazing up at Apollo. Two swans swim in water at their feet and gray clouds collect in the background.
