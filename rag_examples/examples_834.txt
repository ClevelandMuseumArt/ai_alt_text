A gold cross features a central dark-blue sapphire. On each of the four arms of the cross is a square with a figure made from cloisonné, strips of metal outlining the shapes and filled with colorful enamel, most of it now broken away. The top square features an eagle, the bottom an angel, the left a winged lion, and the right a winged ox. Precious stones circle the sapphire and outline these squares, with fine gold filigree between them.
