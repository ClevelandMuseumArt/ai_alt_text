A horizontally oriented oil painting in flat swaths of solid color depicts two women with light skin tones seated outdoors, one painting at an easel and the other reading on a bench encircling a tree to our left. The tree reaches leafy branches over the scene, providing a medium-green background over light-green grass. The woman painting wears maroon and brown and the woman reading a light purple shirt tucked into a peach- and green-striped skirt.
