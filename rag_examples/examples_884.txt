A horizontally oriented hanging scroll depicts people with light to medium skin tones gathered around Tejaprabha, the Buddha of Radiant Light, who, slightly larger and higher than those around him, sits cross-legged in the center. He wears red, green, and blue robes, his left hand grasping a disc in his lap, his right hand raised, with a green halo behind his head. Three rows of people in similarly colored robes flank him, those with medium skin tone, like Tejaprabha, also have green halos.
