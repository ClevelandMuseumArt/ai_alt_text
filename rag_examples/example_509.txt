A darkened green bronze sculpture depicts the Goddess Durga, standing facing us with four arms, a circular item in each hand save for the lower left from which hangs a cylindrical pole curving in to connect with the rectangular base on which she stands. At the center of the base is a bull's head over which Durga, wearing a squared skirt and no top, stands. She has elongated ears, smiles faintly, and wears a headdress.
