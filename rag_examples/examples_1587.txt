A horizontally oriented drawing in charcoal and black crayon closely crops around the head and shoulders of a Black woman angled looking out to our left. The woman, Nefertiti, has medium-dark skin tone and thick, curly, shoulder-length hair shaded black. Thick strokes of crayon outline her features in contrast with the smoother, lighter charcoal shading of her face. Loose, charcoal strokes occasionally extend beyond her defined features, especially in the loose swirls of her hair.
