A brass ewer with a wide body and thinner neck topped with a lid connects to a handle curving down to one side as a cylindrical spout juts upward diagonally from the other. The decoration is highly worn, and silver inlaid shapes piece together scenes of people and animals within scalloped medallions around the ewer's body, against a pattern of winding lines and vines. An inscription runs around the tapered body's slightly wider shoulder (see "Inscriptions").
