A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts four views of women wearing ballet tutu skirts, bending down to lace up their shoes. Their faces and bodies are in hazy gray-and-white coloring that contrasts with red-orange hair and occasional green blotches. Left to right against a blotchy, orange-brown background, one sits, legs out, squatting over her right leg. Next to her, another squats, leaning to her right leg, looking to our left. The third squats in profile facing our left, and the fourth sits in a chair, back to us. Sketchy, black lines outline each position.
