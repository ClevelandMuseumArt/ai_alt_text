A bronze sculpture depicts three Buddhists seated on lotus pedestals next to each other. The deity on our left has his right hand raised, holding a small spherical object, and his left hand near his stomach, his palm facing up. The deity in the middle has short hair with circular curls and hands that are similar to the deity on our left, except his left palm is facing us, with his middle finger bent to touch his thumb. The deity on our right has his hands down, with his right hand resting on his right leg, which is dangling off the pedestal.
