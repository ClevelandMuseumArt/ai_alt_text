A painted wood sculpture depicts the Virgin Mary holding Christ's dead body in her lap. Mary has light skin tone and wears purple robes and a white-gray cloth over her head. Her arms wrap around Christ's waist. He has shoulder-length curly hair and his light skin tone has taken on a gray pallor as his head tilts away from Mary, mouth open, right arm flopping down. He wears a gold and orange wrap, blood seeping from holes in his hands, feet, and the gash in his side.
