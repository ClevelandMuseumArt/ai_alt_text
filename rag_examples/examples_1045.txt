A handscroll depicts twelve sections showing people with light skin tones and different lengths of wiry or curly black hair. Dressed in jagged-edged gray and white rags, they engage in different activities such as eating and playing a drum, one on their knees, one holding a baby, and four appearing with animals. Columns of black Chinese calligraphy appear to the left of the people and a row in larger, blocky characters appears on the right (see "Inscriptions").
