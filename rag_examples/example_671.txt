A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts abstract, nude, round-limbed and bodied figures with green, blue, orange, red, and cream skin tones spread evenly across a black background, red streaks of blood dripping from severed heads and limbs, sometimes into vials. At the center sits a coffinlike box with a cross laid over a white figure within and a blue cross standing above, and over which straddles a skeleton. Many figures bear rows of white teeth.
