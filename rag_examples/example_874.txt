A tempera painted central section of an altarpiece depicts the baby Christ wrapping his arms around the Virgin Mary's neck, their cheeks pressed together. Mary wears a blue robe, also wrapped over her head, and edged in brown-red. Christ wears a brown robe wrapped with a gray-blue wrap and looks up at Mary while she looks out at us. Angels outlined in wavy lines float against the gold background in the upper corners. All have light skin tones.
