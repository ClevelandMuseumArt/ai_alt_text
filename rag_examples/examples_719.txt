A square tapestry depicts people with light skin tones wearing muted blues and reds harvesting a golden field of wheat with a thatched house in the background. Two people, one moving in from our left, the other facing away from us in the center wave a scythe in each hand. Beneath an apple tree in the lower right, a woman sits pointing to coins, a man leans over a table to rest his hand on top of the coins, another person writes in a book standing behind. Behind, horses draw a cart toward four farmers gathering bundles of wheat stalks.
