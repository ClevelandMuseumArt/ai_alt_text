A splotchy green discolored bronze sculpture depicts Ganesha, a plump deity with a human body, elephant head, and four arms each holding a different object, standing on a platform. He swings his hips to his right, body sparsely covered with strips of ornamentation and a cone-shaped crown on his head. His right tusk has broken off, held in his lower left hand. His lower right hand holds a sphere which touches his trunk.
