A vertically oriented oil painting in pink tones depicts six people with light skin tones. A muscular bald nude man leans against a wall, food and glass beside him as he looks at two nude young women, one fixing her hair and the other bending over. Behind, two more young nude women stand, one stretching, the other leaning against a wall. A final person in yellow crouches in the corner, wrinkled face looking to the side.
