A horizontally oriented tempera painting depicts jockeys riding white and brown horses racing toward the left. A crowd of people with light skin tones wearing red-orange, light-orange, and dark-blue robes gather along the edges of the street. Across the background, squared gray buildings with red-brown roofs extend with rows of arched windows out of which lean more viewers. The scene is intercepted by gilded triangles at each corner, decorated by foliage patterns with a fleur-de-lis in the center.
