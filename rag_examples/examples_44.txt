A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a seated woman with light skin tone turned toward us, eyes downturned, wearing a loosely draped, golden dress, tied at the waist with a cord and a blue cloth draped behind her. Her brown hair is pulled loosely back with a twining pearl and cord headpiece. Her right elbow rests on her chair's back, fingers pressed to her forehead, left hand covering a bird in her lap. In the lower left sits an incense burner on a rug, and on the table to the woman's right are flowers in a vase.
