A horizontally oriented tapestry depicts a garden scene with people with light skin tone surrounding a central man wearing an orange cloak and looking toward a woman wearing a dress of the same orange, a man in red sitting at her feet and more people gathered behind them with a white castle in the distance. Three children a fifth the size of the central man play among water running by his feet. At the right, a youth raises a club toward a bearded man with a cane and another person standing on his shoulder. An Old French inscription cuts in to the upper center (see "Inscriptions").
