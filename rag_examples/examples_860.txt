Twelve circular medallions connect to form a necklace by parallel gold chains extending across the top and bottom of each medallion, interspersed with small pearls. In the central medallion, a white enamel woman sits on a gold-petaled flower. Each medallion has a band of gold leaves and pearls encircling it. The remaining medallions feature flowers made from green, purple, pink, and blue precious stones. The medallion over the clasp is squared and slightly larger.
