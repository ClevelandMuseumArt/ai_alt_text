A white-brown marble sculpture depicts Jonah, a man with a bushy beard, emerging from the mouth of a sea monster, head first. The sea monster has a canine head and front paws, wings, and a serpent body, its winding tail flipping over its head, in contact with Jonah's right hand. Jonah sticks out of the creature's mouth from the waist up, hands thrown in the air, his left hand broken off.
