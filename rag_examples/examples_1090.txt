A sculpture represents a stem of cranberry or lingonberry stood up in a transparent glass with a cone-shaped body and cylindrical neck. The berries have a gold stem branching into two sections with teardrop-shaped, jade green leaves. At the top, five berries cluster on the left branch, and four on the right. Spherical, the berries are milky white, washed out red, or dark red. The glass appears filled with water up to the neck.
