An orange-brown wood sculpture depicts a woman seated cross-legged with a child across her lap, her right hand resting over the child's legs and the left supporting the child's neck. The woman has long, thin breasts that jut out from her body. Her only adornments above the waist are thin bands around her upper arms and neck and above her breasts. Her eyes are carved as outlines, and her mouth is slightly open, revealing rectangular teeth.
