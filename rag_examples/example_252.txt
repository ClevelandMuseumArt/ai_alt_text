A vertically oriented oil painting in blue tones depicts the bustling Fifth Avenue city street, created with loose flecks of paint. The street cuts diagonally across the painting from the lower right, buildings rising in the upper half and streaks suggesting swarms of people and cars moving through the lower half. Beyond blue, brown, and golden yellow fleck the buildings and people closest to us while the crowds thicken toward the back, becoming more of a solid, misty white and blue.
