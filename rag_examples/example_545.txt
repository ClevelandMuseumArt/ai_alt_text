A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts people with light skin tones out hunting in orange, green, and blue clothing against blotchy, black-inked rock outcroppings. In the upper center, two people ride on horseback from our left, a falcon flapping its wings on the first rider's outstretched hand while two more people run before them. Lower, three others with horses, two mounted, one holding the reins, surround a person kneeling on the ground, pulling two birds apart.
