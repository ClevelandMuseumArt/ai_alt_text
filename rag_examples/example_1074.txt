A handscroll depicts pastel green and blue mountains interspersed with trees nearly the same height with brown trunks and needle-like leaves. Loosely suggested people roam in robes at the mountain's base, including two people and two white deer, a person crossing a bridge on our left, and two people and two cranes in a clearing on our right. A red-robed person sits on a crane flying through a dip in the mountains, upper center.
