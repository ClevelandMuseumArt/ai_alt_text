Mixed media on a vertically oriented canvas features a blotted, blue-green background and a central cream doily surrounded by various painted organic shapes. An oblong orange blob is painted below the doily, a streaking brown blob to the left from which extends black numbers. Painted above is a figure like a deconstructed, highly abstract elephant, with a stick-figure in a diagram to the right. A vertical line of half yellow, half black almond shapes runs down the right side.
