A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts animal heads transitioning to leaves, floating against the cream-colored paper background. Birds scatter around the painting on the lower and upper edge. In the lower center, leafy stalks sprout from an elephant head, and an ibex, cow, deer, big cat, and more surround, some biting at leaves. A lion, leopards, and donkey heads create a circle in the center left. Colorful birds bend in a circle around gold just above.
