An oval oil painting depicts a woman with light skin tone, shoulders turned slightly to our left but looking out at us with light brown eyes and a faint smile. She wears an elaborate dress with multiple layers of sheer, white collar fringed in lace, a red-petaled flower at the center of her chest, and three rows of pearls around her neck. Her brown hair, slightly gray at front, is pulled back, with a circular gold and jewel ornament in it, while she wears a matching, triangular earring.
