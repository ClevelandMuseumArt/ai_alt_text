A discolored green bronze sculpture depicts Shiva, a god with four hands, standing on a pedestal, his hips swinging to his right. He holds a rearing antelope figure in his upper left hand and an axe in his upper right, his lower hands making gestures. He has long ears, wears a conical, densely patterned headdress, and is sparsely dressed in snaking garments and jewelry. An ornament like a face rests at his waist.
