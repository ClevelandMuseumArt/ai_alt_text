Two earthenware sculptures depict figures with remnants of burnt orange, red, blue, and yellow coloring, wearing large headdresses and layers of jewelry and sitting cross-legged on domed bases. The figure on the left has monkeylike features, including bared teeth and a curling tail. Both figures hold their right hands up, as if working on the mask held in the other hand by the left figure or the bark piece held by the right figure.
