A metal incense burner is shaped like a stylized cat and patterned with Arabic script (see "Inscriptions") and flowing, intertwined lines. The animal's four legs stick straight down, tapering to the feet, and support a cylindrical body and neck that curves into a round face. The face, mouth open with circular holes for eyes, has vertically pointed ears with spheres on top. A cylindrical tail curves to run parallel over the horizontal body, curling upward just before the back of the neck.
