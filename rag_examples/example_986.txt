A gray-brown wood sculpture depicts a standing humanlike figure leaning on their right foot. They have a contorted expression with furrowed brow and smooth wood where the mouth would be. They raise their right hand, fingers splayed and palm out, and place their left fist against their hip. Fabric wraps around their waist. Their ribs show on their bare chest despite muscular arms and legs. Their hair is in a knot atop their otherwise smooth head.
