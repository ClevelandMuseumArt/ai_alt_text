A beige, stucco sculpture depicts a monk standing looking to our left. In both hands, he holds a roughly circular object detailed with an almost honeycomb-like texture. He has a worn face and close-cropped hair. The stucco, a fine, grainy plaster, forms the faintly undulating folds of a robe extending to where his feet have broken off. Rough edges show in particular where his left side would have fused with a larger background.
