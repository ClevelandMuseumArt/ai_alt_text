A rectangular box depicts a dragon and a phoenix in inlaid mosaic-like mother-of-pearl pieces glinting pink and green covering most of the lid's dark-brown background. The dragon curves up, individual pieces highlighting its scaly body as well as the tail-feathers of the phoenix, spreading its wings. The tail-feathers transform into leafy vines, extending onto the box's side, while spiraling patterns extend around the dragon. A solid field of mother-of-pearl pieces surrounds these figures.
