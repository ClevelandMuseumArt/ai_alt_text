A horizontally oriented print in black ink on two sheets of beige paper joined at the center depicts Christ and the twelve apostles, all with light skin tones, wavy hair of differing lengths topped by halos, seated at a "U"-shaped table. Sometimes holding knives and goblets, they sit behind the table, looking around the multicolumned, ornately decorated room, save for Judas. He sits on a stool to the right of the seam, turning back to look at us, a bag clasped in his left hand while Christ raises his right hand in blessing on the left side of the seam.
