A handscroll in black ink features staggered sections of Chinese calligraphy in the left four-fifths (see "Inscriptions") and a depiction of bamboo and orchids sprouting from a central rock in the final fifth. Sparse, streaking strokes outline the rock, while dark strokes highlight the bamboo's narrow, pointed leaves. Slightly lighter, long, narrow strands splay from the rocks. Pointed petals surround the loose strokes of the interior of the orchids nestled among the bamboo.
