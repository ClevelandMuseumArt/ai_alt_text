A mixed-media sculpture looks down a cartoonlike rendered New York street bustling with a yellow cab, bus, and red car flanked by people on sidewalks jutting out front. Behind, teetering buildings narrow before meeting at a point. An orange car carrier and polka-dot cement truck pass each other at a streetlight in the middle-ground. Signs in puffy lettering say things such as "COFFEE" and "DAVES LUNCHEON CORNER" as they jut out from the multicolored buildings.
