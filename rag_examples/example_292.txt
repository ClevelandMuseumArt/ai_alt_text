A wood totem pole depicts carved figures with squared bodies, placed one above the other and painted red, brown, orange, and cream. The figures have wide mouths, furrowed eyebrows, and tuck in their knees and elbows. Stacked from bottom to top are two otters, an owl, a whale with a popsicle-stick shaped fin gripped by another figure, the downward facing mouth of another whale, the pointed beak of a raven, and a small oval-shaped figure at top.
