A rotund terracotta piggy bank is shaped as a wild boar with small tusks lifting the edges of its snout, eyes slanting downward, brow furrowing. It has pointed ears and a band like a chain wrapped around the neck, which merges seamlessly with the hump of the back. The tan-brown colored hump transitions to the body, a darker brown with black speckles, then hooves, dotted with red, black, and orange.
