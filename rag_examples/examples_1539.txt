A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts an abstracted, geometric still life colored primarily blue, gray, and brown. A central shape represents a white, stemmed bowl in which sit three green circles. Left stands a rectangular, blue bottle over which arcs a red, geometric shape. Right, thin, rectangular, white biscuits stack on a white plate. Leaf patterns sporadically mark the table, some solely outlined, others green. The paint is sometimes thick, sometimes cracked, and sometimes sand-textured.
