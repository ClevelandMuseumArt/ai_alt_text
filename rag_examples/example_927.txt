A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Polyhymnia, a muse with light skin tone wearing a muted blue dress layered over with a mustard-yellow drape across her left shoulder as she holds both arms to our right while looking at us. A gold crown in her black hair holds a white veil billowing behind her, her mouth slightly open as her left foot steps forward. A chair sits in a marble niche behind her, a gold staff leaning against it, and a column rising above upon which the bust of a bearded man sits.
