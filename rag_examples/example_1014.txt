A vertically oriented opaque watercolor painting on light yellow-brown paper depicts Shah Tahmasp, a man with a light skin tone and a finely detailed graying beard, kneeling on a carpet in a sparse landscape. He wears a light blue robe with a gold floral pattern over a light purple shirt, a white turban twisting to a point on his head. An oblong orange pillow with a pattern of wavy gold shapes sits behind him. A thin-branched willow tree arcs above him.
