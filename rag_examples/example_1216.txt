A vertically oriented oil painting closely crops around a person throwing their head back as they raise a clear, stemmed glass to their lips with their left hand and hold a clear carafe in their right. The person has light skin tone and wears a crumpled white shirt. The carafe has a spherical body half filled with an orange-brown liquid and a cylindrical neck as tall as the body. Streaking, white brushstrokes emphasize light catching on the glass.
