A beige limestone stele, or stone panel, features three bands carved with various flattened figures, all in profile. The central and largest band centers a seated god with a crescent and disk headdress. Two winged flanking goddesses frame him, making a diamond shape with their wings. Two figures stand to the right, heavily worn, and another to the left. In the band above, baboons bow toward a central crescent and disk. The band below features a row of figures. Hieroglyphics, a pictorial writing system, appear throughout.
