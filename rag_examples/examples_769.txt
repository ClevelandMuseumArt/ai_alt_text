A mustard-yellow helmet features a fin on top and a dark-brown corselet, an armor vest. The corselet has flat panels connected by chainmail across the chest and in three rows of squares below, creating a skirt-like shape. The chainmail juts out in triangular edges at the shoulders. Two organically-shaped clasps hold either side of the chest together. The helmet visor tents up at the center, fin and edges outlined with a serrated pattern.
