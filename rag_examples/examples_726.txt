A horizontally oriented tapestry depicts a garden scene with people with light skin tones wearing ornate garments of various shades of pink and dark blue, and a white castle in the upper right, next to a block of Old French text (see "Inscriptions"). A central woman wears a light-orange dress, arms spread to the seven people flanking her, some playing instruments, while two adults and a child a fourth her size move through flowers at her feet.
