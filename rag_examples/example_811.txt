A horizontally oriented oil painting divided horizontally depicts water in the lower half and a clear, blue sky in the upper half, with a cliff jutting halfway across the composition from the right. The cliff has a peach, cream, and yellow streaking face and undulating grass green top. All these colors streak among the fragmented blue strokes of the water as its reflection. Black specks of people stand along the narrow beach. Peach sand dunes undulate on the left horizon.
