A portable, ornate, golden altar features Christian figures with halos standing in archways on the side panels with gems and pearls running around the border. Various enamel blue, red, white, and green geometric patterns decorate the archways, contrasting with the solid gold figures within them. On this visible side, Christ stands in the center, flanked by three people on either side. The top of the altar is made from a sheet of porphyry, a white-speckled, purple rock.
