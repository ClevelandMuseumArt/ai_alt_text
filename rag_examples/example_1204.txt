A square oil painting depicts four circular scenes against a black background. At upper left, a woman thrusts a sword down a frog's throat, birdlike creatures rising behind her. At upper right, a nude white-haired woman rides a bird while others hold skulls. At lower left, women crouch over a small flame, a skeleton hovering above. At lower right, a man gestures with a stick toward a long-necked creature floating upward. The lower two circles are cast mostly in darkness.
