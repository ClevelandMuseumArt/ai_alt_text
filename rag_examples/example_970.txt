A horizontally oriented print is densely covered with white and light pink-purple irises. The irises have thin leaves in green brown shades that narrow to a point. The flowers have some small petals sticking up while larger ones droop down on the sides. The white petals blend into one another, appearing like fluffy clouds winding between the leaves and pink-purple flowers. Only on the far left do arching, long leaves transition into the plain beige paper.
