A gray-brown iron suit of child's armor survives in partial condition. The edge of each metal sheet is lined with rivets capped with gold circles. The helmet includes a gold face guard with vertical bars. The armor extends over the thighs to the knees, with layered metal sheets giving a centipede-like effect, mimicked to a lesser extent on the shoulders and elbows. Armor is missing for the right hand and for both legs below the knees.
