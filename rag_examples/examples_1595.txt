Roughly rectangular leather has been completely impressed and carved with repeated, abstracted renditions of the artist, one merging with the next. He holds a sledgehammer, wearing a horizontally, black-and-white striped jumpsuit, a black hat, and has a dark skin tone. Sometimes his back turns to us, other times he faces us, features detailing his face. The hammers have brown handles and dark gray heads, except one in the upper left with a light gray head.
