A handscroll features tightly packed Chinese calligraphy on its left half and a group of sixteen people standing gathered before the Buddha on its right half. The Buddha sits cross-legged in a pink robe on a lotus throne, flanked by four people. The people gathered before him wear different combinations of robes and trousers and have various colors of hair and hairstyles. One steps forward, bowing his head and holding out an incense burner.
