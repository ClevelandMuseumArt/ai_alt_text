A brown handscroll depicts a line of Chinese calligraphy on the right, and a scene of stylized, muscular people in black ink processing along the left four-fifths of the scroll. They have knobby limbs and wiry hair, many bare-chested and some baring their teeth. People gather on the left side of the scroll, three supporting the robe-wearing, bearded Zhong Kui on their shoulders as he raises his right hand, looking over the procession. More play instruments behind him.
