A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Clio, a muse bracing a rectangular tablet on her knee. She reaches out as if to write on it while lookingout to our left. Feathered wings extend from her back, open and transitioning from white feathers to brown. She has a light skin tone and wears a laurel wreath and yellow and light-purple garment. Surrounded by busts, reliefs, and sculptures, a column and trees stand behind her.
