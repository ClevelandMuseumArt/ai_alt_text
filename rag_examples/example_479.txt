A folding screen features eight vertically long panels with white rectangles framing peony flowers in the upper two-thirds. A light brown subtle abstract flower-and-dot pattern extends through the lower third. Branching up and clustered among green leaves, the frilled layers of the peonies are colored red, purple, yellow, and blue with each petal edged in white, except for the red peonies, edged in a lighter orange-red.
