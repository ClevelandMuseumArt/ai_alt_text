A roughly rectangular sculpture made of fine, clear glass tubes glowing white as they catch the light and webbing creates multiple levels of geometric shapes. The central level features a "U"-shape made three-dimensional with another "U"-shape glowing within it. This is suspended by rectangles that create a box around it, each wrapping around a side, with a gap between them in the middle. They lift the "U"-shape up, as it also extends above their edge.
