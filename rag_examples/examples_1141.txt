A light brown stone sculpture, roughly rectangular in shape, depicts Shiva sitting beside and looking at Parvati, figures a fifth their size clustered around. Shiva's left leg folds up, his right hanging off the bench pressing into a platform below at which a ten-armed demon crouches, flanked by more figures in a symmetrical composition. Shiva has a halo behind his head, his and Parvati's faces worn. The sculpture's upper left corner has broken off.
