A large, vertically oriented tapestry with faded colors is worn at the top and edges. At the center of the tapestry is the Virgin Mary in blue garments with a child Christ in her lap, seated on a throne, and flanked by two angels. Above that is a depiction of a man flanked by two angels and surrounding both depictions are smaller portraits of religious figures that frame the edges of the tapestry. All figures are light-skinned.
