A medium-brown leaf-shaped wood paddle is covered in carved patterns. Columns of a grid pattern, each box filled with an "X" run across the paddle, arc around the edge, and extend down the handle. On the handle, they alternate with rows of crescents. Each section of pattern is separated by rows of triangles. Squatting abstract human-like figures, tiki tiki tangata, enwrap the cylindrical base. Curving crescents outline eyes and tooth-like triangles jut down like teeth.
