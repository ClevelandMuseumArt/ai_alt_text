A vertically oriented oil painting depicts God holding Christ's limp body, surrounded by angels, all with light skin tones. A white dove hovers in a circle of sky above God's head. God wears a domed blue and gold crown and red and gold robes and Christ wears solely white cloth wrapped around his waist. Two flanking angels swing incense burners. Others rise into the clouds, holding the instruments of Christ's Passion, such as the cross and spear.
