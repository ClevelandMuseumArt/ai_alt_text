A medium-brown leaf-shaped wood paddle is covered in carved patterns. On the paddle, rows alternate between a grid pattern filled with "X"s and rows of crescents. A single row of repeated radiating circles runs horizontally across the center. The "X" pattern also cuts vertically down in a strip at the center. On the cylindrical handle, rows of radiating circles and upside-down triangles alternate. Squatting abstract human-like figures, tiki tiki tangata, enwrap the flared base.
