A square tempera painting depicts Saint Catherine of Siena, a woman with light skin tone wearing a black and white nun's habit, a halo around her head, and depicted twice in two scenes. On the left, she looks up, hands in prayer, at Christ, with light skin tone appearing waist-up out of a gold cloud, one hand raised in blessing, the other holding out a dark cloak. On the right, Saint Catherine hands the cloak to a man with medium-light skin tone and sparse clothing holding a cane. White, squared columns extend behind these scenes.
