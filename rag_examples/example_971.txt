A dark brown, wood sculpture depicts Zaō Gongen, a deity who bends his right knee up, right arm held up at a right-angle and left bent down, two fingers pressed against his waist. His right hand holds a cylinder with three prongs on each end. He wears a feline animal pelt wrapped around his waist and a piece of cloth on his left shoulder. Eyebrows furrowed, a flame-like shape extends from the top of his head.
