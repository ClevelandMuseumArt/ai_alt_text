A vertically oriented oil painting features fractured geometric shapes in muted pastel pinks, purples, and blues with a central silhouette in purple of a winged figure riding a horse. The horse dives down, legs extending through the air, a star just over its head and a wavy, cream strip extending from their nose and trailing behind them. The winged figure riding the horse braces themself with one hand on the horse's mane and the other on its hind.
