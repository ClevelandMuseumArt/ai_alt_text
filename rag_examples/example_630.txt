A rectangular panel is pieced together from marble and other stone in primarily browns and dark blues into a depiction of a two-columned building flanked by two arches. Center steps lead to a doorway above which is a ladder symbol and above that a crown nestles into a triangular roof. On either side, representations of menorahs, seven-branched candelabra, sit on black-and-white checkered tile over which hangs a cabinet with books or scrolls in them.
