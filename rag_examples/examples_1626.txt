A beige, stucco sculpture depicts a man standing looking to our left and subtly crouching. In both hands, he holds a roughly circular, rough-edged object. He wears a rounded cap, his face in particular marked by the wear of gashes in the stucco. The stucco, a fine, grainy plaster, forms the faintly undulating folds of his robe, which extends to where his lower legs have broken off.
