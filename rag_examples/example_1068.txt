A hanging scroll in black ink depicts Lin Bu, a man with light skin tone, walking to our right and looking up at a gnarled plum tree in a rocky landscape. Lin Bu, wears a robe and has a sparse mustache and goatee. Over the tree fine swirls layer and streaking branches in darker shades, alongside dark dots and streaks on the tree and rocks. Text extends down from the upper left (see "Inscriptions").
