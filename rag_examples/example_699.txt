An orange ceramic nikosthenic amphora vessel features a wide body narrowing to a base. A cylindrical neck extends from the top with two wide, flat handles arcing to connect neck and body. On the body dance a band of alternating satyrs, men with horse ears and tails, and women. On the upper body alternate lions and sphinxes, with two dancing youths on the neck. Vertical stripes and geometric patterns alternate with the figural bands.
