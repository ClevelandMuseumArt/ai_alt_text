A vertically oriented oil painting with thick brushstrokes and dollops of paint features two central orange and green poplar trees against a blue sky and swirling muted green, blue, and cream hills. The side-by-side trees are bushy and narrow, thinning toward the top, with the left tree taller than the right. A squared house can be seen in the background through the gap between them. Dark blue paint sketchily outlines the landscape.
