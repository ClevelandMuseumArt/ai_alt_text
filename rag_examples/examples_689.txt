A black ceramic bell-krater vessel features light orange-tan detailing and a pot with two horizontal handles. A central scene depicts a woman in a flowing garment, bracing herself with one hand as she leans back on a stool and holds a mirror in the other. The winged god Eros stands nude in front of her, holding a ribbon draped over both hands. A pillar behind the woman reads "ΑΦΡΟΔΙΤΗ," or "APHRODITE."
