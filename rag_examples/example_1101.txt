An ornately carved yellow-brown wood armchair features light blue floral-patterned upholstery on the back, arms, and seat, including a fluffy pillow on top. White floral patterns edge the upholstered sections while various pink, yellow, and white flowers bunch in the center. More floral patterns have been carved into the chair's wood structure. Carved, lionlike heads protrude in front of the upholstered arms, curving down and into a spiral where they meet with the legs.
