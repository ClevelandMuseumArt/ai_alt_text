A finely embroidered vertically oriented silk textile depicts two carp, dark and light brown-gray scaled fish, twisting among swirling dark blue water. The scales of the fish are outlined in light gray and the swirling currents of waves in white. Smaller, gold swirls and clusters of different-sized circles wind around the carp. On the lower right, one carp wiggles down, while on the upper left the other curves in a "C" shape, looking up.
