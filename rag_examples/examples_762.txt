A greenstone, a smooth, dark-green rock tinged with brown, depicts a highly abstract creature-like shape. The head, almost as big as the body, tilts to our left, with carved out circles as eyes, a diamond shape between them, and a beak-like mouth, somewhat worn. This figure has a rectangular torso, legs spread out the side, and arms curving down to merge by the hips. Where details have been carved out, the rock appears gray.
