A horizontally oriented oil painting depicts people with round faces and light to medium-light skin tone crowded among paintings in a room. In the center, an artist holds up a painting that another sits in front of, inspecting. At left, a person in a luminescent white gown sits in front of a person at an easel, both looking at us. Through an archway on our right, people sketch, encircling a nude person. Paintings and drawings line the walls.
