A vertically oriented painted wood panel fragment depicts flattened people in profile. A mummified man and woman with medium and light skin tones stand facing our left, their bodies tightly wrapped. A woman with light skin tone crouches in front of them, reaching for the man's wrapped legs. At left, a man with medium-dark skin tone wearing pleated white and spotted animal skin garments holds out two objects. Hieroglyphics, a pictorial writing system, extend in columns above.
