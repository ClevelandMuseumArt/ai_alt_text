A square silk cloth is embroidered with a white bird in flight, looking up to a small, circular, red sun. Stylized clouds swirl around the bird, mostly blue but some red and yellow, with arcs of waves in shades of blue below, all against a gold-flecked black background. A band of bronze-colored rectangular spirals against black runs around the edges of the cloth. Flowers and red batlike creatures are interspersed among the clouds.
