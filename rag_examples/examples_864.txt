A tempera painted altarpiece with a central panel depicts a baby Christ wrapping his arms around Mary's neck and two smaller, flanking panels. The left panel depicts Saint Andrew crucified, with St. Francis and St. Paul below and the right, Christ with the instruments of the Passion, such as a crown of thorns and spear, above and St. Stephen and St. Lawrence below. Each person has light skin tone and is framed against a gold background.
