A vertically oriented oil painting creates the face of Paul Cadmus entirely from diamond shapes filled with organic, multicolor rings. Cropped tightly around his face, Cadmus has light skin tone and blue eyes, looking out at us. Organic rings in pastel purples, greens, and oranges fill the diamonds shading the lighter portions of his face, darkening at creases around his eyes and mouth and his eyebrows. Blues and browns color his eyes and shirt collar.
