A vertically oriented oil painting features Christ crucified on a cross, a dove perched above his head, wings open, and the larger-scale God standing behind him, wearing the cone-shaped papal crown and an ornate gold cloak. A sheer red with fine gold lines radiates behind him, through which cherubs are visible. In the corners sit angels, hands clasped in prayer. All have halos and light skin tone save for God's medium-light skin tone.
