A horizontally oriented watercolor painting depicts seven, identical people with medium-light skin tones arcing across off-white paper. They have black bob hairstyles with "X"-shaped headdresses. Their eyes and mouth are shaped as white circles from which extends a line on either side, outlined in black. They wear red shoes, yellow and black shirts and socks, white skirts with geometric patterns on the left side, and fern-like foliage around their necks and in their left hands.
