A vertically oriented and highly fragmented linen textile depicts a stylized half-nude man and nude woman with light skin tones standing under an archway. The woman leans back against the man looking up at him as he, wearing a green wrap speckled with black dots, looks down at her. Bits of the textile have deteriorated, most noticeably across their chests and along the edges. To their left stands the remnants of a blue column with a brown capital.
