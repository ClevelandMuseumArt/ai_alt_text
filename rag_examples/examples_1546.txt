An abstract vertically oriented black-and-white photogram depicts a leaf in the upper two thirds and upside down pipe in the lower third from which extends a bubble. The photogram, made by placing objects onto a light-sensitive material and exposing it to light, creates an  x-ray-like quality. The pipe extends in horizontally from the lower right. Split down the middle, the leaf has two roughly triangular, down-pointed symmetrical halves. The leaf is jagged along the upper edges.
