A horizontally long strip of papyrus, a striated, paperlike material, features scenes intermixed with columns of black-inked hieroglyphics, a pictorial writing system. At left, scenes include a black jackal, a doglike creature, and the jackal-headed god Anubis standing over a mummy. Then, there are two rows making fifteen rectangles, or gates, framing mostly animal-headed, knife-bearing creatures. Right, stands a person almost the height of the papyrus, gesturing back to the rest of the papyrus.
