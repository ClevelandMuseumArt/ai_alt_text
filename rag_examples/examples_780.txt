A carved wood festival pole painted red, black, and white, depicts a standing figure, arms hanging by their sides. Their head is colored black and marks the top. They have wide, semi-circle eyes, a painted black unibrow, a wide nose, and teeth pressed together showing both rows. A narrow, braid-patterned goatee hangs down from their chin, bisecting a bird-like face with a humanlike body just below their head. Both figures' left hands have broken off.
