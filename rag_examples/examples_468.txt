A vertically oriented black ink print depicts a nude Venus, a goddess with light skin tone, stepping out of the ribbon-like waves onto a seashell, her back to us but turning to her right so we see her face, eyes closed, gathering her wavy hair at the nape of her neck with her left hand. Above her, in clouds in the sky are two crowned bearded men with light skin tones, one brandishing a sword toward the other, reclining.
