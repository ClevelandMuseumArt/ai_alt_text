A bronze situla, a waterdrop-shaped vessel, opens to a cylinder and features a circular handle hooked through loops on either side of the rim. A scene outlined in fine, white lines decorates the body with gods standing single file in front of a table filled with objects, a person holding out a cup across it. They face our right, the god Osiris central from this angle, holding a crook and staff, and falcon-headed Horus behind.
