A kris, a sword with an asymmetrical, triangular base, extends into a straight, silver metal blade with a rounded tip. The blade's triangular base cuts off into jagged edges on the right and a circle over triangle pattern punched out of one edge of the left. A scalloped edge transitions to alternating black with cream dots and vice versa in vertical sections, reminiscent of dice.
