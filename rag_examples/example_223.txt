A necklace features two pouches at either end shaped like half-ovals with white, pinks, and browns in striped and triangular patterns made of miniscule beads. Strands of predominantly pink, blue, and white beads hang down around the pouches. Beaded strands extending from either side of the flat edge of each pouch connect the pouches into a loop. The beaded swaths alternate between white, green-brown, and deep blue separated by cylinders beaded with zigzag and diamond patterns.
