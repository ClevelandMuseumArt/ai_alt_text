A horizontally oriented tempera painting depicts people with light skin tones gathered to the right of a gray-white brick tower. Directly in front of the tower, the Virgin Mary wearing vibrant blue, holds baby Christ in her lap, Joseph wearing a yellow robe to her right. Christ places his left hand on the head of one of the three Magi who is kneeling, bent at his feet while the other two line up behind, all wearing ornate, gold garments. All people have solid gold halos. An entourage of people and animals extends behind. A cave-like alcove with a manger, cow, and donkey extends into a gray-hilled background.
