A handscroll in black ink features Chinese calligraphy on the left half (see "Inscriptions") and a rocky mountain landscape on the right. A central, diagonal ridge of rocks is flanked by two shallower ridges, one on the right is covered with branching trees adorned with smudges and loose circles as leaves. Smaller streaks of trees and foliage adorn the central ridge, while the furthest left is bare. Red stamps intersperse with more text.
