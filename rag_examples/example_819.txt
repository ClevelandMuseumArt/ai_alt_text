A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a woman with light skin tone reclining on a couch in front of a window. Her red-brown hair is in a bun and she wears a frilly, white dress with layers and adorned with yellow bows. She leans her right elbow on the floral-patterned couch, leaning her head against her hand, while her left arm flops over a mustard-yellow pillow at her side. A red-striped awning extends from the window, beyond which runs a sandy beach with thin strip of blue water and two figures carrying umbrellas at the left.
