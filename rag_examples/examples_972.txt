An orange-brown earthenware sculpture is shaped as a stylized horse with four cylindrical legs, a short, pointed tail, and a rectangular, flat snout. The horse has a bridle over its face and a saddle on its back, the band running in front of its chest decorated with small spheres, two larger spheres just behind the saddle. A mane sticking up like a fin runs between two pointed ears. Cylindrical holes in the face represent the eyes.
