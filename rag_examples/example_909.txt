A cylindrical, cast bronze bell curves in slightly at the top around which runs a spiked rim like flower petals. An abstract dragon writhes on top, mouth open, with a cylindrical tube behind. Below the petals runs a band of squared spirals from which two squares extend down, spirals in their borders and three rows of three flowers inside. On the lower end of the bell there is a circle with more shapes inside. Along the lower rim runs a band like flowers in diamond shapes. The bell has corroded green with flecks of gold.
