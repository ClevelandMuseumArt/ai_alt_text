A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts the Buddha with gold-tinged skin seated cross-legged on a stylized lotus flower with multicolor petals. Two standing figures flank and look toward him. Above each of these figures are two more seated figures and then three more cross-legged, each half the size of the figures below. Below the lotus, a row of three standing figures flank a seated one. These figures have gold-tinted, blue, red, and other colorful skin tones.
