A barong, a single-edged, leaf-shaped sword, features a striated, splotchy silver metal blade. A cylindrical bronze handle extends up, capped with a dark brown and gold piece, curving to the left. A narrow strip extends across the top in an arcing diagonal line, the upper right side with an organic-shaped serrated lower edge. Around the piece's body runs a pattern of alternating yellow diamonds, and yellow dot outlined diamonds, a yellow band running below.
