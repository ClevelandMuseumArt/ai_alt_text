A square, gilt plate depicts three people softly hammered out from the back of the sheet. In the center, the Medicine Buddha sits cross-legged on a lotus-shaped pedestal, flanked by two standing people facing them. They wear clothes draped around them in many folds and have halos behind their heads. The flanking people press their hands together in front of their chests. The Buddha cradles a small object in his left palm. Spirals wind above their heads.
