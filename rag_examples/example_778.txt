A barong, a single-edged, leaf-shaped sword, features a striated silver metal blade occasionally tarnished with specks of dark-brown. A cylindrical bronze handle curves to the left side and is covered in warping, organic designs. It is capped with an ivory colored piece extending up in a diagonal line, patterned with organic curls above the shorter side and with three lines below. Three braided bands wrap around the upper half of the brown handle.
