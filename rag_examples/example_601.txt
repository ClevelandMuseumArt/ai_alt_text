Two side-by-side vertically oriented tapestry fragments depict alternating patterned bands with red backgrounds divided by strips of blue wavy shapes on yellow. A row of four pink, wiry figures with white specks for eyes, teeth, and nails adorn one band. The next band, double in height, features a central square with two extensions on either side supported by flanking creatures that look like insects. Further geometric creatures surround the structure. Yellow, green, and light-blue colors dominate.
