A vertically oriented frenzied mosaic made up of multicolor fractured tiles features predominantly square, black tiles outlining a central figure's square head and shoulders. Solid oval eyes and a rectangular mouth occupy most of the face with colorful tiles cutting in, particularly green squares in the mouth. Below, the shoulders dissolve into reds, blues, and yellows with predominantly white tiles constructing the figure's background. Red, yellow, teal, and black soap or wax scrawls over the tiles.
