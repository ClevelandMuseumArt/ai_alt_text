A vertically oriented oil painting depicts the angel Gabriel, his wings like peacock feathers, kneeling before the Virgin Mary, seated on a throne and dressed in rich blue robes, both with light skin tones, against a gold background. Mary looks down at a stand, a scroll rolling over the edge, as Gabriel points up. In two blue circles in the upper left corner, a bearded man with light skin tone gestures toward Mary, a small, nude baby with light skin tone shooting down toward her.
