A vertically oriented oil painting depicts colorful flowers sprouting from a beige vase located at the lower two-thirds of a yellow-brown background. Thick daubs create the flowers, large red ones around the vase's rim, and finer light-purple, light-yellow, and white weaving throughout. Thick, dark-green leaves splay out from the center of the flowers. Two feathery-petaled, yellow flowers extend from the top right and thick orange petals extend against the blue lower left corner, next to the vase.
