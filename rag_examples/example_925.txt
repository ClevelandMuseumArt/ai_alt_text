An oil portrait from the waist up depicts Saint Catherine of Alexandria, a woman with light skin tone seated facing our left but turning to look out at us with brown eyes and small lips. She wears a pink, long-sleeved dress and grasps a spiked wood wheel on her lap. A green, feather-like palm branch leans from the crook of her right arm. A silvery veil covers her brown hair, cascading down her back.
