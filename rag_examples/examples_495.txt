Two black-and-white side-by-side repeated square photographs depict a Hindu temple tank, a reservoir holding water. In the water, front left, sits an open-air island structure with a roof supported by columns, a tower with carved figures and capped in a pyramid shape. To the right, on land, extends the taller, ornate temple gate with a square shape narrowing on top. Further right, a wagon carries a structure reminiscent of the gate, but made of sticks.
