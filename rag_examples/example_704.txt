A black ceramic kylix, a drinking cup, features a wide bowl, flanking horizontal handles, and a narrow stem flaring at the base. Orange figures outlined in fine, black lines and with black hair twist in different positions as they line up around the bowl. The figures alternate between women wearing rippling tunics, and nude satyrs, men with horse ears and tails. Zigzagging fine cracks indicate where the cup fragments have been pieced back together.
