A horizontally long beige linen cloth embroidered with dark blue that provides a background to a central eight-point star flanked by two smaller stars. These stars appear flower-like with primarily red, yellow, blue, and green shapes. The blue makes its own abstract star-like shape, before extending into a solid field of blue interrupted only by two flanking patterned squares in the same colors, from which extend two outlines like quill nibs pointing in toward the stars.
