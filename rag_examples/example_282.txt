A circular ceramic mask is painted burnt orange with dark red and black detailing. The mask has holes for eyes, a narrow, vertical strip jutting out as a nose, and a thin, curving up mouth incised with squares like teeth. A smaller figure with a similar face extends from the forehead and is flanked on each side by three rectangular knobs where black serpent heads jut out after snaking across the face. Below the mouth are three, more rounded knobs.
