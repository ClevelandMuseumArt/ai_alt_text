A vertically oriented tempera painting depicts people with light to medium-light skin tones in the foregrounded palace and background battlefield. Topless women swim in a central square pool, surrounded by a symmetrical, multilevel palace adorned with yellow, orange, and blue patterns and flowerbeds. Receding into the background, a horizontally running river with boats divides the palace from fields with rows of people bearing weapons. At the upper center, an ombre blue circle cuts into the scene.
