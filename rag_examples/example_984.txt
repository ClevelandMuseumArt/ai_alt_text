A hanging scroll depicts various scenes against a dark blue background, the cracked surface and smeared paint obscuring details. Above and center, three gold deities sit cross-legged in front of a sprawling building, with three, gold, standing figures a third their size below. Center left, people wrestle against a swirling, red background. Further people, with medium-light to light skin tones, become harder to decipher but run holding weapons and sit in grass, among other things, below. A house sits in the lower left corner.
