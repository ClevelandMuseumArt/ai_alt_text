The front and back of a drawing are displayed on our left and right, respectively. On the left, a highly abstract watercolor with fine black lines creating the barely identifiable resemblance of a figure. In the background, grainy yellow and red fade to grays, while the shapes made by the fine black lines are occasionally colored with solid black, red, blue, or yellow, as well as a white cresent moon and two blue stars. On the right, a pencil, stick-figure-like drawing on cream, water-stained paper of a figure bending back, words written in the jagged-edged circle of their hair (see "Inscriptions").
