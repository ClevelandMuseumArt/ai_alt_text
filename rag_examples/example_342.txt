A horizontally oriented watercolor with chalky, opaque gouache in orange, blue, yellow, white and gray depicts the town Flüelen blurred across a lake. In the lower right, loose lines create a boat floating from orange-bathed waters into blue, toward the briefly sketched buildings, a single spire visible, of the town on the horizon. A mountain rises on the left and gray sky curves over the town, before dissolving into yellow sky on the right.
