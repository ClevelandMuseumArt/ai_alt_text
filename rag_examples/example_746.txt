A vertically long yellow silk textile depicts an embroidered skinny dark brown tree with winding branches sprouting pink and blue flowers and long, green leaves. These cover the entirety of the fabric except for the rectangular border, where the repeated pink flowers are interspersed with smaller pink and blue ones and brown and green vines with blue and pink bows. The bows imitate the larger one tied around the tree trunk, with double blue ears, a pink knot in the middle, and speckled with white dots like the tree.
