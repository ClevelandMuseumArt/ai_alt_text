A vertically oriented oil painting depicts a wineglass, bowl of fruit, and section of carpet emerging from a shadowy background. They are clustered on a silver platter on a stone table at the right. The bowl is blue-patterned white china holding a speckled orange and two gray-green peaches. The wineglass has an ornate gold stem with a silver child supporting the glass at the center. Liquid partially fills the glass and a shorter glass behind.
