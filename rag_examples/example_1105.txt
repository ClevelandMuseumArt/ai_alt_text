The back of an oval-shaped hand mirror features a central, smaller, gold oval depicting a nude Venus, a goddess with her hands in her hair, rising out of blue waves. Four semicircular arches on each side provide gaps between the central image and the turquoise blue enamel frame patterned with yellow, pink, and white flowers and green leaves. The mirror has a plain beige rectangular handle and clusters of spheres sprouting in even intervals around the edge.
