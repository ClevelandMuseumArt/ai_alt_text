A hanging scroll depicts the corner of a terrace wrapping around a house on which people with skin tones the same color as the beige-brown paper engage in various activities. In the doorway, a person plays a koto, an instrument like a plank of wood with strings. Sitting on the terrace, two people play go, a board game. In front of the terrace, one person stands reading and another lies on their side, painting. The people have black hair and wear black, white, and red robes more defined than the faint lines of the house and tree. A golden-yellow color weaves in and out of the ground and sky.
