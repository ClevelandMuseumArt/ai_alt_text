A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Clio, a muse with light skin tone seated with her legs facing our right but looking to our left. Feathered wings extend from her back, open and transitioning from white feathers to brown. She wears a laurel wreath in her black, wavy hair, a yellow and blue garment, and braces a rectangular tablet on her knee, reaching out as if to write on it. A column and trees stand behind her. Busts, reliefs, and sculptures scatter around her.
