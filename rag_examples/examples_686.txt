A light brown and reddish orange ceramic neck-amphora vessel features a wide body narrowing to a smaller base and a neck about as long as the body with two snake-shaped handles connecting the neck to the body. Continuous geometric zigzag and square patterns intersperse with bands depicting repeated abstract, thin-lined figures. On the neck, people raise their hands to their hair, on the body are two-horse drawn chariots, and, below, scrawny doglike creatures lie down.
