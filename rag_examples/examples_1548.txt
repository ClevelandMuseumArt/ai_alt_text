A mottled orange-brown wood table features a roughly rectangular tabletop supported by a column shaped like a lyre. The lyre, a U-shaped stringed instrument, has five gold bars as strings down the center. It sits on a rectangular base with four legs curving out from it and capped with gold lion faces and wheels where they meet the floor. Further gold details adorn the table, including spiraling flower-like shapes on the lyre and table corners.
