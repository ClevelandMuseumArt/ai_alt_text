A gilt silver chalice features an ornate stem and lower half of the cup and smooth, gilt upper half. Angels, depicted as faces flanked by wings, wrap around the lower cup and appear above and below a representation of Christ, a man in a robe, carrying a cross in the stem's center. The circular base features scenes of Christ's suffering and death, interspersed with more angels. Foliage motifs and scale-like patterns fill any remaining space.
