A vertically oriented oil painting depicts Diana, a goddess flanked by nymphs, all with light skin tones, and two dogs. At the far left, a satyr, a man with medium-light skin tone and goat legs, grabs a nymph, leaning to kiss her as she shoves him away. Diana holds a spear and reaches down to touch a black-and-white dog looking up at her, front paws on her leg. She wears a tiger skin and a red garment uncovering one breast.
