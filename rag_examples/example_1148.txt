A black bronze sculpture with green discoloration depicts the Buddha seated cross-legged on a serpent. He sits on three scaled rows of a coiled serpent tail while a cobra hoodlike shape rises behind his head with a large, central serpent head and three smaller ones flanking either side. The Buddha folds his hands over each other in his lap, a small, patterned, egg-shaped object in his upturned palm. He has long ears and looks straight out.
