A vertically oriented geometric oil painting depicts a highly abstracted coffee mill, bottle, and wine glass. They cluster on a dark green table cloth over a red-brown table which is angled towards us. The coffee mill has a wood-grain patterned base with a faint, white outline of a crank on gray above it. The angular narrow-necked bottle and squat wine glass fracture into white, grays, and blacks with touches of teal blue and dark green.
