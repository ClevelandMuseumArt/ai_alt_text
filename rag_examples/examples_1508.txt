A brown bronze sculpture depicts a muscular man pressing a cylindrical drill half their height into the roughly circular base he stands on. Knees bent, he bends over the drill, pressing down on the top with his left hand, his left elbow pointing up. He extends his right arm to place his hand on the base. He has close-cropped hair and is shirtless, wearing trousers, gloves, and shoes pushing into the sculpture base's undulating surface.
