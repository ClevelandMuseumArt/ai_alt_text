A sculpture of a woman in dark brown wood with orange undertones is seated with her legs in front of her and hands bracing either side of a bowl placed between her shins. Her lips turn slightly upward, carved eye shapes with a line through them conveying closed eyes. Diamond patterned marks on her chest and arms also encircle the rim of the bowl and its lid. The woman wears a necklace of indigo, coral, and white beads.
